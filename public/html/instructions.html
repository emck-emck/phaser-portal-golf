HOW TO PLAY

OVERVIEW

This is a mini-golf simulator that allows for the use of teleportation portals.

PUTTING

Use the left mouse button to putt the ball

Click and hold on the screen and drag in the direction opposite where you want the ball to go, then release.

PORTALS

Use Q to shoot the orange portal and E to shoot the blue portal.

Going through one portal brings you out the other portal.

Portals can only be shot on to white walls.

PORTALS

The ball must have line of sight to the wall you want to place a portal on.

OTHER CONTROLS

Press W to cancel a putt.

Press F to toggle Full Screen mode.

Press P to pause the game.

HAZARDS

Sand: Slows ball significantly.

Water: Sinks ball; ball returns to previous putt position.

ITEMS: CUBE

Push the ball into a cube to move the cube on a horizontal or vertical axis.

ITEMS: PRESSURE PLATE

Putt the ball or move an item onto a pressure plate to trigger an event.

ITEMS: OTHER

Other items include:

Moving Walls

Disappearing Walls

Bridges

OBJECTIVE

Complete the course in as few strokes and as little time as possible!
